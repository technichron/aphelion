APHELION ISA 2.0

8-bit word length
16-bit address space



REGISTERS (8-bit)

0b0000 A general
0b0001 B general
0b0010 C general
0b0011 D general
0b0100 E general
0b0101 F flags
0b0110 G general



DOUBLE REGISTERS * can be used as 8 bit registers by suffixing 'l' or 'h' to the register name. if no end is specified, 'l' is assumed.

0b1000 I general
0b1001 J general
0b1010 K general
0b1100 P program counter
0b1101 S stack pointer
0b1110 R return/link pointer
0b1111 Z zero	* always contains zero


INSTRUCTIONS


nop - no instruction
mov reg, imm16
mov imm16, reg
mov reg, reg
mov dreg, dreg
